My journey so far

All my academic and professional experience with some milestones achieved are summed up here:

Finalists-Flipkart's Grid 2.0 Reached finals of the Grid 2.0, Robotics track, organised by Flipkart by being among top 9 team amongst 1500 teams nationwide. Presented a novel, low-cost and highly efficient intelligent picking mobile robot for warehouse management. See certificate
Jan 2021
First Research Paper Published My first research paper titled "Multi-objective Bonobo Optimizer (MOBO): an intelligent heuristic for multi-criteria optimization" was published in Knowledge and Information Systems, Springer Journal. Refer my google scholar profile
Aug 2020
Research Intern at UCL As a part of the Surgical Robot Vision Group at the Wellcome / EPSRC Centre for Interventional and Surgical Sciences (WEISS) with Dr Agostino Stilli, I worked on modularising the reinforcement learning simulation environment for da Vinci Surgical Robot (dVRK). See certificate
Apr 2020
Terrace Farming Robot Won Gold Medal at Inter-IIT Tech Meet 8.0, held at IIT Roorkee in December 2019. The Inter IIT Tech Meet 8.0 Robotics event was organized in partnership with DIC, Roorkee. The problem statement was to build a robot to perform basic farming operations such as ploughing, sowing, harvesting and watering, and scale stairs (terrace) of 40 cm in hilly regions. The team developed a fully autonomous robot with a novel stair climbing mechanism (to be patented). See certificate
Dec 2019
Research Intern I worked as a summer intern from 6th May 2019 to 15th July 2019 at Soft Computing Laboratory, IIT Kharagpur under, Prof. Dilip Kumar Pratihar. I worked on developing three versions of Multi-Objective Bonobo Optimizer to solve multi-objective optimization problems with constraints. The algorithms were tested upon 30 benchmark problems and compared with four other well-performing algorithms from literature. The results are compiled and is published in Knowledge and Information Systems, Springer Journal. See certificate See codes
